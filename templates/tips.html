Thinking Ahead: 5 Proven Tips and Tricks for Chess Mastery

  1. Play with a Purpose

    2. Always have a clear goal in mind when making a move. Whether it’s controlling a key square or setting up a tactical opportunity, play with a purpose. *Chess is a strategical game ―make yourself a favor and learn the Best Chess Strategy.

  2. Keep Your Opponent’s King in Mind

    3. Just like your own king, your opponent’s king is a critical piece. Keep it in mind and look for opportunities to attack it.

  3. Pay Attention to Piece Coordination

    4. A well-coordinated group of pieces is much stronger than individual pieces on their own. Look for ways to coordinate your pieces to maximize their effectiveness.

  4. Control the Diagonals

    5. The diagonals are important paths for bishops and queens. Try to control them as much as possible to limit your opponent’s options.

  5. Use Your King as an Attacking Piece

    6. In the endgame, your king can be a powerful attacking piece. Don’t be afraid to use it to help push your pawns or attack your opponent’s pieces.
